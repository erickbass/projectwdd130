The Catarat of Lodore

..The caract strong

Then plunges along,

Striking and raging

As if war waging Its caverns and rocks among;

Rising and leaping, Sinking and creeping

Swelling and seeping, Showering and springing

Flying and flinging, Writhing and ringing,

Eddying and whisking, Spouting and frisking

Turning and twisting

Around and around Whit endless rebound;

Smiting and fighting, A sight to delight in;

Confounding, astounding, Dizzing and defeating the ear with its sound
